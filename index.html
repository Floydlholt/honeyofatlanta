Honey of Atlanta

Honey of Atlanta; the finest in natural organic gourmet honeys from the surrounding Geogria areas.

All of our honey is produced and bottled in Georgia. Our honey is raw, 100% pure, unheated, uncooked, unfiltered and natural. You will see natural pollens floating in our honey, because we use no pumps, pipes or filters when extracting and hand-bottling our pure, local Georgia. We believe in the natural and healthy properties of raw honey and never want to remove any of them from our product. Local honey is not only loaded with antioxidants, but it is also great for the prevention of allergies and is the healthiest alternative to processed sugar.

Our business is our bees. Honey of Atlanta is a family owned and operated apiaries located in Middle Georgia/East Atlanta areas. Head Beekeeper and owner Floyd Lewis Holt, is a dedicated first generation beekeeper from Middle Georgia. He started with a single beehive in 2017. In 2019 He created Honey of Atlanta and began to sell honey at Farmer Markets around the Atlanta and Macon areas. Together with friends, family and the bees we strive to provide the community with the highest quality of local raw honey.
