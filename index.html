Who or what is
Honey of Atlanta?

Honey of Atlanta is more than just selling raw and local honey, it’s made in backyard hives—maybe even your neighbor’s yard! That’s our concept for Honey of Atlanta. We integrate bees into the local ecosystem through a network of backyard hives instead of lumping them together on some distant apiary. The bees live as they naturally would and their benefits reach more people. Our goal is to raise Atlanta’s bee population throughout the city and at the same time spread awareness among the community. Our hope is that these backyard hives will facilitate a dialogue among neighbors, friends, family and the community at large about the importance of bees
